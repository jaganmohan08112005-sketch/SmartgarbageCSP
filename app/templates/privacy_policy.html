SmartGarbage Chintalavalasa ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how long we keep it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name, phone number (validated as Indian mobile), role.
  • Waste declarations: weights of wet/dry/sanitary/hazardous waste, ward, timestamp.
  • Reports & complaints: text, optional photo, GPS location you provide, status.
  • Bin telemetry: fill level, temperature, methane, battery — sent by IoT sensors, not by you.
  • Audit logs: administrative actions (who did what, when).

2. Why We Collect It

To operate waste collection, generate PAYT invoices, display ward transparency dashboards, comply with SWM Rules 2026 reporting, and keep the system secure.

3. Anonymous Illegal-Dump Reporting

Reports submitted via /report-illegal (and the WhatsApp/Telegram bots) are stored without any user account linkage. We intentionally do not retain the reporter's identity.

4. Data Retention

  • Waste declarations & invoices: retained for the current financial year + 1 year.
  • Audit logs: 2 years (security & compliance).
  • Photos: compressed and retained only as long as the associated report is open or under review.

5. Your Rights

You may request correction or deletion of your account data by contacting the Gram Panchayat office. Anonymous reports cannot be linked to you and are therefore not retrievable.

6. Contact

Chintalavalasa Gram Panchayat, Vizianagaram District, Andhra Pradesh, India.

This is a demonstration deployment. Treat all data as test data.
